FAQs

  • Which CAPA transactions are eligible for 6% tax?

    All the transactions of CAPA tokens done by users like buy, sell or transfer actions, are eligible for 6% fixed tax. This tax is infact used for the welfare of CAPA token holders, to reward them multifold.

  • How is paying 6% tax beneficial for the holders?

    The collected tax is thoughtfully divided into three equal segments, each serving a distinct purpose. One-third is directed towards token burning, increasing scarcity and intrinsic value. Another third is converted into $CAKE tokens, forming a rewarding pool for loyal CAPA token holders. The final third is allocated to the CAPA Treasury for essential activities such as liquidity boosts and strategic marketing initiatives.

  • What is CAPA Meme Nation?

    CAPA is not just another token; it's currency of CAPA meme nation, that brings together the vibrant world of memes, the efficiency of blockchain technology, and the spirit of community collaboration.

  • How can I earn rewards from CAPA protocol?

    CAPA protocol rewards biweekly and annual rewards to the token holders. To earn these rewards you just have hold CAPA tokens and stay active in the ecosystem by following the 15 days tax compliances. Besides, there are other trigger events and opportunities in the Panda Thunder to earn rewards.

  • How do I participate in the CAPA Meme Nation?

    To become an active participant of CAPA Meme Nation you just have to hold CAPA tokens in your wallet. You do not need to deposit your tokens out of your wallet. The protocol reads your token activities from your wallet.
