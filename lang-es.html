Frequently Asked Questions

  • Which CAPA transactions are subject to the 6% tax requirement?

    All CAPA token transactions conducted by users, such as buying, selling, or transferring, are subject to the fixed 6% tax requirement. In fact, this tax is utilized for the benefit of CAPA token holders, rewarding them in various ways.

  • What are the benefits of paying a 6% tax for holders?

    The collected tax is evenly divided into three equal parts, each serving a different purpose. One-third is used for token burning, increasing scarcity and intrinsic value. Another one-third is converted into $CAKE tokens, forming a reward pool for loyal CAPA token holders. The final one-third is allocated to the CAPA Treasury for crucial activities, such as enhancing liquidity and strategic marketing initiatives.

  • What is CAPA Meme Nation?

    CAPA is not just another token; it is the currency of CAPA Meme Nation, bringing together the vibrant Meme world, the efficiency of blockchain technology, and the spirit of community collaboration.

  • How do I earn rewards from the CAPA protocol?

    The CAPA protocol distributes rewards to token holders every two weeks and annually. To receive these rewards, you only need to hold CAPA tokens and stay active in the ecosystem, following the 15-day tax compliance requirement. Additionally, there are other triggering events and opportunities in Panda Thunder to earn rewards.

  • How do I participate in CAPA Meme Nation?

    To be an active participant in CAPA Meme Nation, you simply need to hold CAPA tokens in your wallet. There's no need to store the tokens outside your wallet; the protocol reads your token activities from your wallet.
